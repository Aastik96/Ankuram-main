Dear Parents and students,

I feel great to welcome you to "Ankuram' family. The thought process behind bringing Ankuram to you was brought up by my grandfather Pandit Hariomji Dave. His concept was simple ‘How to provide world class education in Jhabua, Madhya Pradesh’. With this thought, I have extensively researched, visited many schools and have gathered opinion and guidance from different educators.

Finally, 2 years ago, we started our journey of providing education, from Ranapur. To facilitate the concept, we chose the best teachers.

It is worth to mention that at that time we were the only school in the area to provide homeschooling during the covid 19 pandemic. Hence, we undertook expansion activicites so that there was no effect of the pandemic to the learning of our students and no compromise was made even during covid second wave.

Our efforts towards providing quality education has progressed to another level and now we are proudly presenting a state of art school. This structure has been built under the guidance of well experienced and master educator Dr Ritesh Limaye.

Dr Ritesh Limaye has an experience of over 21 years in handling school affairs and as principal various prestigious educational institutes along with dedicated team of teachers.

With all the efforts from our team and guidance of expert educators, I am confident that Ankuram will prove to be a benchmark in providing quality education in Jhabua and will be able to impart world class education to the students.

Dr Lokesh Dave
MD Homeopathy
Founder
